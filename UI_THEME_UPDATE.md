## 💡 Design Highlights

### Color Usage
- **Yellow (#ffc300)**: Primary actions, highlights, branding
- **Black (#000000)**: Main background
- **Dark Gray (#0d0d0d)**: Cards and elevated surfaces
- **White (#ffffff)**: Primary text
- **Muted Gray (#cfcfcf)**: Secondary text

### Spacing
- Consistent padding: 20px, 24px, 32px
- Card gaps: 24px
- Form field spacing: 20px

### Shadows
- Cards: Deep shadows for depth
- Buttons: Elevated on hover
- Inputs: Subtle focus glow
